## About the Collection

This collection will be a group of images that was taken of or by Walter Bennett throughout his life from 1950 to 1964. 
This collection will showcase two distinct periods of Walter’s life. The first being his life and experiences while serving in the Army Command Triste United States Troops, or TRUST, in Italy along the Morgan Line between Italy and Yugoslavia. The second period being his life in the decade or so following his military service in which he settled in Richland, South Dakota with his wife Marilyn and their growing family. 
All images featured in this collection are digitized versions of photo slides that belong to the Bennett family. Many of these images have only been seen just a handful of times as they were in storage for nearly fifty years. 

## About Walter Bennett

Walter Bennett was born on June 1st, 1926 in Sioux City Iowa. From 1950 to 1952 he served for the US Army in Zone A of the Free Territory of Trieste, Italy, where he was stationed near the Alpine Foothills. 
After returning home from his military service, Walter married Marilyn Meeves in January 1953. The couple settled in Richland, South Dakota, where they began raising their family. Together, Walter and Marilyn had four children: Scott, Tari, Steve, and Stuart.
Walter Bennett passed away surrounded by family and loved ones on September 11th, 2009.
