## About the Collection

This is a collection of digized scans of photoslides that Walter Bennet had taken while stationed in Merano, Italy from 1950 to 1952 and images of his life once he returned home. 

- a CSV of collection metadata
- a folder of JPG images or PDF documents

## Collection History

This collection of digitized slides was the first time that many members of the Bennett family had seen these images in over fifty years. This collection uncovered family history, never before seen images, and gave additonal context to where Walter Bennett was serving in the Army.

The template repository features four objects from the University of Idaho Library's [Digital Collections](https://www.lib.uidaho.edu/digital).
